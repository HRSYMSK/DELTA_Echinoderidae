Short description

Species with middorsal spineson segments 4, 6 and 8, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9, lateral accessory tubes on segment 8 and laterodorsal tubes on segment 10. Glandular cell outlets type 2 present in subdorsal and lateral accessory position on segment 2. Females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Lateral terminal spines long and thin. Sieve plate small.
