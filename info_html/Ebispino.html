Short description

Slender species with middorsal spines on segments 4 and 6, ventrolateral tubes on segment 2, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, andlateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9. Glandular cell outlets type 2 present on segment 2 in subdorsal and sublateral positions (not included in original description). Lateral terminal spines acicular, and females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

The species is quite easily recognized by its dorsal spine pattern. The presence of middorsal spines on segments 4 and 6 only, is only present in E. bispinosus, and its close relative E. astridae. The two species are most easily distinguished by the sublateral tubes on segment 8, present in E. astridaebut absent in E. bispinosus.
