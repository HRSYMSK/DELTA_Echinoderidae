Short description

Species without middorsal spines, lateroventral tubes on segments 5 and 7-9, midlateral tubes on segment 8 and laterodorsal tubes on segment 10.
Small glandular cell outlets type 2 present insubdorsal, laterodorsal and ventrolateral position on segment 2, subdorsal position on segment 4, midlateral position on segments 5- 7, and laterodorsal position on segment 8. Lateral terminal spines short and stout, andfemales with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate large.

Key characters and similar species

lacking any spines and having tubes on segments 5, 7-9, and 10, short and stout lateral terminal spine, and large sieve plates.
The absence of any spines on together with the presence of large sieve plates clearly assigns the species to the Echinoderes coulli species group.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from its type locality, muddy sediment at intertidal flat in the Ishigaki Island, Japan.
