Short description

Trunk with middorsal spines on segments 4, 6 and 8, sublateral tubes on segment 2 (not mentioned in the original description), lateroventral tubes on segment 5, and lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9. Glandular cell outlets type 2 absent. Lateral terminal spines thin and long, and females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.
Tergal extension short triangular (not mentioned in the original description).

Key characters and similar species

The re-examination of the type of the species revealed thatthe species possesses sublateral tubes on segment 2. In addition, the species doesnt have rounded tergal extension but has short triangular tergal extension. The spine/tube pattern of the species is unique among the Echinoderidae

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from thepolymetallic nodule fields at the Clarion-Clipperton Fracture Zone in East Pacific Ocean, at 4 121– 4319 m depth.
