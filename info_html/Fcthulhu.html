Short description

midodorsal acicular spines on segments 4-8, ventrolateral tubes on segment 2, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral acicular spines on segments 6-9, laterodorsal tubes on segment 10; type-2 gland cell outlets present in midlateral position on segment 8 andlaterodorsal position on segment 9; short acicular; female with ventrolateral papillae on segment 7 and lateral terminal accessory spines;. Sieve plates small.

Key characters and similar species

can be distinguished most of echinoderid species by having the completely-divided tergo-sternal junctions on segment 2. This character is shared only with Fissuroderes species. Among the Fissuroderes species, F. cthulhuis similar to F. novaezealandiaand F. thermoiin the spines and tubes pattern. However, F. cthulhu differs from R. novaezealandiain having the laterodorsal tubes on segment 10, and differs from F. thermoiin the pattern of the type-2 gland cell outlets.

Distribution and habitat

. cthulhuis known from Mahavavy area, Mozambique Channel, western Indian Ocean at 775-789 m depth.
