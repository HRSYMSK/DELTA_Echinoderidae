Distribution and habitat

The species was described from muddy sediment collected at 32 m depth in Orcas Island in the San Juan Archipelago, Washington, USA. Posteriorly found in several localities in Calvert Island, Canada in sandy and muddy sediments ranging from 88-350 m depth.
