Short description

Segment 2 consists of one tergal and two sternal plate divided bylateroventral and midventral articulations.
Trunk with middorsal spines on segments 4 to 8, ventrolateral tubes on segment 2, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9. Glandular cell outlets type 2 present insubdorsal position onsegment 2, laterodorsal position on segments 5, 6, 8 and 9. Lateral terminal spines thin and long, and females with ventromedial pores/papillae on segment 7 and lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

The species is easily recognized by the presence of middorsal spines on segments 4-8, and the pattern in the type-2 gland cell outlets.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from at 527 -1468 m depth west off New Zealand.
