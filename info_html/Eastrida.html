Short description

Slender species with middorsal spines on segments 4 and 6, ventrolateral tubes on segment 2, lateroventral tubes on segment 5; lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9, and sublateral tubes on segment 8. Glandular cell outlets type 2 present on segment 2 in subdorsal and sublateral positions. Lateral terminal spines acicular, and females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.
