Short description

Species with middorsal spines on segments 4 to 8, lateroventral spineson segments 6 to 9, laterodorsal tubes on segment 7, and subdorsal tubes on segment 9. Glandular cell outlets type 2 unknown.
Lateral terminal spines long and thin.
Presence/absence oflateral terminal accessory spines in females unknown. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

The species is no longer confused with other congeners based on its spine/tube pattern, especially the presence of the laterodorsal tubes on segment 7 as well as the subdorsal tubes on segment 9.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known onlyfrom its type locality, the sediment of grey mud at 22 m depth in the Ulsan Bay, Korea.
