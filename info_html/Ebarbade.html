Short description

Species with short middorsal spine on segments 4 to 8, segment 2 with tubes in ventrolateral position, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9 andlateral accessory tubes on segment 8. Type 2 glandular cell outlets presentin subdorsal position on segment 2 and midlateral position on segment 4. Lateral terminal spines short and stout, and females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate large.

Key characters and similar species

The species is easily recognized bythe combination of spine/tube pattern and short and stout lateral terminal spines. Together with these characters, the presence of the large sieve plate help identify the species.

Distribution and habitat

The species is only known from its type locality in Barbados, Caribbean Sea.
