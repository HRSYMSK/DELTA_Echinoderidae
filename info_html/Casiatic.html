Short description

Segment 2 of the species withlateroventral articulations and midventral partial articulation. The trunk withmiddorsal spines on segments 4 to 8, segment 2 with tubes inventrolateral positions, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9, lateral accessory tubes on segment 8 and laterodorsal tubes on segment 10. Glandular cell outlets type 2 absent. Lateral terminal spines thin and long, and females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from Peter the Great Bay (type locality), Russia, from estuary muddy sand at 8 m depth; and the Akkeshi Bay, Japan, from muddy sand at 13 m depth.
