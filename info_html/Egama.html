Short description

medium length middorsal spine on segments 4 -8; lateroventral tubes on segment 5; lateroventral spines on segments 7 to 9, sublateral tubes on segment 8, and laterodorsal tubes on segment 10. Type 2 glandular cell outletsin subdorsal and lateroventral positions on segment 2. Lateral terminal spines acicular, and females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

The species is easily recognized by its spine/tube pattern, especially thepattern of the lateroventral acicular spines present on segments 7-9. This pattern is unique and known only in thisspecies.

Distribution and habitat

The species is only known from its type locality in the Daidokutsu submarine cave, Okinawa, Japan.
