Short description

Species with middorsal spines on segments 4 to 8, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 8. Glandular cell outlets type 2 unknown. Lateral terminal spines short stout.

Key characters and similar species

The speciescan be recognized easily by its middorsal spines on segments 4-8, lateroventral spines only on segments 6-8, and short and stout lateral terminal spines.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from its type locality, the greymud sediment at 22 m depthin the Ulsan Bay, East coast of Korea, the Seaof Japan.

Literature
