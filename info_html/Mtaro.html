Short description

Segment 2 with lateroventral incomplete articulations, but these articulations cannot be detected in some specimens.
Trunk with middorsal spines on segments 4 to 8, lateral accessory tubes on segment 5 (theoriginal description mentioned the tubes on lateroventral position), lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9, and laterodorsal tubes on segment 10. Tubes on segment 2 absent (wrongly mentioned in the original description).
Glandular cell outlets type 2 absent. Lateral terminal spines thin and long, and females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

Among the all Echinoderidae species, the species similar to E. bathyalis and E. dubiosus in the general shape, spine/tube pattern and length, and tergal extension shape. However, M. taro can be distinguished from them in the absence ofany type-2 gland cell outlets.

Distribution and habitat

polymetallic nodule fields at the Clarion-Clipperton Fracture Zone in East Pacific Ocean, at 4127 – 439 m depth.
