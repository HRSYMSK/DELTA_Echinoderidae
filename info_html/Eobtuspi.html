Key characters and similar species

The species shows identical spine and glandular cell outlet type 2 pattern with E. pennakiand E. remanei/E.svetlanae/E.tubilak.
However E. obtuspinosuscan easily be distinguished from the rest by its short, stout and rigid lateral terminal spines.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from several localities at Jeju Island, South Korea, collected from intertidal algae and from material washed out of a hermit crab. Potential new recordings from shallow muddy areas in the Northwest coast of Canada are currently being examined (Maria Herranz unpublished records)
