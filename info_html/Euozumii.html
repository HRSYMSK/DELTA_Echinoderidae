Echinoderes uozumii

Echinoderes uozumii Yamasaki, Fujimoto & Tanaka, 2020

Short description

medium length middorsal spines on segments 4 and 6; lateroventral tubes on segments 2 and 5; lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9, andlaterodorsal tubes on segment 10. Type 2 glandular cell outlets insubdorsal and lateral accessory position on segment 2. Lateral terminal spines acicular, and females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

Distribution and habitat

The species is only known from its type locality in the Daidokutsu submarine cave, Okinawa, Japan.
