Short description

Species with middorsal spines on segments 4, 6 and 8, segment 2 with laterodorsal tubes, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9 and laterodorsal tubes on segment 10. Glandular cell outlets type 2 absent. Lateral terminal spines thin long. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

Although the spine/tube pattern is unique among the all known Echinoderes species, the species has not been named because only a single specimen available so far.

Distribution and habitat

The species was recorded from slope of Senghor Seamount, northeastern Atlantic Ocean, at 565 m depth.
