Short description

Segment 2 consists of one tergal and two sternal plate divided bylateroventral and midventral articulations.
Trunk with middorsal spines on segments 4 to 8, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9. Glandular cell outlets type 2 present in subdorsal position on segments 2 and 9, and laterodorsal position onsegment 6. Lateral terminal spines thin and long, and females withventrolateral pores/papillae on segment 8 and lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small. Tergal extensions narrowing abruptly into long acicular tip.

Key characters and similar species

The species is easily recognized by the segment 2 comprised of one tergal and two sternal plates, presence of middorsal spines on segments 4-8, and theabsence of any tubes on segments 2 and 5.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from at 2378- 3202 m depth west off New Zealand.
