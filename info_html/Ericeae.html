Short description

Species with middorsal spines on segments 4, 6 and 8; ventrolateral tubeson segment 2; lateroventral tubes on segment 5;

lateroventral spines on segments 7, 8 9; lateral accessory tubeson segment 8 and laterodorsal tubes on segment 10.
Absence of glandular cell outlets type 2. Sieve plate small and displaced to sublateral position.

Key characters and similar species

The species has a unique pattern of spines and tubes, the lack of lateroventral spines only on segment 6 combined with the presence of a single pair of tubes on segment 2 andthe absence of glandular cell outlets type 2 make it very easy to differentiate.

Distribution and habitat

locality 20 miles off the coast of Fort Pierce, Florida, USA collected from mud at 140 m depth.
