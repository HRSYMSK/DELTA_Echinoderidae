Short description

Segment 2 of the species with lateroventral articulations and midventral partial articulation. The trunk with middorsal spines on segments 4 to 8, segment 2 with tubes in ventrolateral positions, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9 and midlateral tubes on segment 10. Glandular cell outlets type 2 present in subdorsal position on segment 2 and midlateral position on segment 8. Lateral terminal spines thin and long.
Females with ventromedial pore/papillae on segment 7 (not mentioned in the original description) and lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

Among Cephalorhyncha, C. polungais the only species with type-2 glandular cell outlets.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from thepolymetallic nodule fields at the Clarion-Clipperton Fracture Zone in East Pacific Ocean, at 4090 – 5012 m depth.
