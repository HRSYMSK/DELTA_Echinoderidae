Short description

midodorsal acicular spines on segments 4-8, ventrolateral tubes on segment 2, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral acicular spines on segments 6-9, midlateral tubes on segment 10; tergal extensions short while, sternal extension round and extending beyond tergal extensions.G landular cell outlets type 2 absent; lateral terminal spines short; females with lateral terminal accessory spines andventromedial papillae on segment 8. Sieve plates small.

Key characters and similar species

a rather common spine/tube pattern, buthas a rare characterof broad sternal plates extending beyond the tergal plates.
This character is shared with only E. vulcanicus and E. krishnaswamyiso far.
And the two species can bedistinguished each other by the presence/absence of type-2 gland cell outlets.
