Short description

Segment 2 consists of one tergal and two sternal plate divided bylateroventral and midventral articulations.
Trunk with middorsal spines on segments 4 to 8, ventrolateral tubes on segment 2, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9, and laterodorsal tubes on segment 10. Glandular cell outlets type 2 present in midlateral position on segments 5, 6 and 8, and laterodorsal position on segment 9. Lateral terminal spines thin and long, and females withventromedial pores/papillae on segment 7 and lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small. Tergal extensions truncated.

Key characters and similar species

The species is easily recognized by the segment 2 comprised of one tergal and two sternal plates, presence of middorsal spines on segments 4-8 andtype-2 gland cell outlets on segments 5, 6, 8 and 9, and the absence of anydorsal tubes on segment 2.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from at 85- 98 m depth east off Costa Rica and at.97-162 m depth in the Gulf of California.
