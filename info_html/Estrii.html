Short description

Species without anymiddorsal spines.
Tubes present in midlateral position on segment 3, laterodorsal and lateroventral position on segments 5 and 8.
Males with additional tubes in subdorsal positionon segment 10. Glandular cell outlets type 2 absent. Females without lateral terminal accessory spines. Lateral terminal spines thin acicular. Sieve plate large. Segments 10 and 11 often retracted into the preceding segment.

Key characters and similar species

The species is unique in spine/tube pattern, no middorsal spines and tubes on segments 3, 5 and 8. Any other species show this spine/tube pattern, and thus, the species is longer confused to other congeners.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from the Pacific coast of Panama, mud withmangrove mud at intertidal zone (type locality).
