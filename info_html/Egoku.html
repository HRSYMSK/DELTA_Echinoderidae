Echinoderes goku

Echinoderes goku Rucciet al., 202 2

Short description

with middorsal spine on segments 4, 6, and 8, and spine in lateroventral positions onsegments 6– 9. Tubes present in subdorsaland ventrolateral positionson segment 2, lateroventral position on segment 5, laterodorsal positionson segments 7 and 9, and lateral accessory position on segment 8.

Male with a laterodorsal tubeson segment 10; females with laterodorsal fringe.

The presence of laterodorsal tubes on segments 7 and 9 is a unique combination, and present in E. gokuexclusively.

Distribution and habitat

Sandy sediment at 40 m depths near Bahia Blanca, Argentina, South West Atlantic.
