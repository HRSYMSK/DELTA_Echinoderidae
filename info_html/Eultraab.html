Eultraab

Echinoderes ultraabyssalis Adrianov & Maiorova, 2019

Species with middorsal spines on segments 6 and 8, ventrolateral tubes on segment 2, lateroventral tubes on segment 5, lateroventral spines on segments 6 to 9, lateral accessory tubes on segment 8, laterodorsal tubes on segment 9. Glandular cell outlets type 2 absent. Lateral terminal spines long acicular. Females with lateral terminal accessory spines. Sieve plate small.

Key characters and similar species

The species similar to E. daenerysaeand E. hviidarumin having the middorsal spines on segments 6 and 8. However, E. ultraabyssalis differs from E. daenerysae in the absence of laterodorsal tubes on segment 2; and from E. hviidarum in the presenceof ventrolateral tubes on segment 2.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known fromthe bottom of the Kuril-Kamchatka Trench at 9411– 9541 m depth.
